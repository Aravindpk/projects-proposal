PS Poshan

Apply
A technological solution to monitor food and milk intake among children 6 to 8 months old, and to recommend adequate complementary feeding practices for mothers in order to prevent child malnutrition. A possible solution would be designing a series of smart utensils which can find the quality, quantity and frequency of food being fed to the child. i.e Smart spoons, smart bowls, smart milk bottles etc.
Looking for proficiency in
Embedded systems, Electronics, Web development, Materials and ceramics, Big data

PS Leaky Pipe - Vellore

Apply
Leaks from pipes, plumbing fixtures and fittings are a significant source of water waste for many households in the city of Vellore. Some leaks are obvious, such as dripping faucets and leaking water heaters. Unfortunately, many leaks go undetected for years because the source of the leak is not visible. Detecting the water leakage in household pipes and determining a tech like leak tracing and or leak mapping for preventing them is the main objective of this project.
Looking for proficiency in
Manufacturing, fluid dynamics, ANSYS simulations,CAD,embedded systems

PS Deaf or Hard of Hearing

Apply
Consider a specific population - Deaf or Hard of Hearing (DHH) individuals. There is considerable research suggesting that native DHH persons (those with hearing impairments from birth/very young age) have poor skills with spoken languages such as English. Looking at projects that would make web text content more accessible to DHH persons via a plugin that provides sign language support in the form of videos or animations. This would aid these users in better understanding information and being more self-sufficient.
Looking for proficiency in
Sign Language, web development and 3D animation (for sign language animations)
More information
http://www.dhananjaih.com/asldictionary.html

PS 7 Vertical Farming

Apply
As urban populations continue to rise, innovators are looking beyond traditional farming as a way to feed everyone while having less impact on our land and water resources. Vertical farming is one solution that's been implemented around the world. Vertical farms produce crops in stacked layers, often in controlled environments, using a process called "aeroponics," which relies on air and mist. Looking at implementation of an affordable and sustainable system in Indian cities.
Looking for
Electronics, Electrical, Computer Science, Mechanical Engineers

Team RoverX

Currently one of the biggest projects of Creation Labs, The Team roverX designs and builds martian rover that can perform specific tasks on mars or similar kind of terrains autonomously. Their primary goal is to participate in the European Rover Challenge (ERC) and University Rover Challenges (URC). They participated in the ERC 2015 for the first time and were ranked 13th worldwide and got better at ERC 2016, ranked 12th worldwide and 1st in Asia. The team is comprised of passionate undergraduate students from various branches such as Mehanical, Electrical, Computer Science, Information Technology and Biomedical Engineering.
